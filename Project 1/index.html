Why Daily Diary?

What is Daily Diary?

Daily Diary is a unique social media platform designed to help you capture and share your cherished memories in a fun and doodly way. You can create short text descriptions, attach photos, videos, or sounds, and add date, time, and location details to each memory card.

How do I create a memory card?

Creating a memory card is a breeze! Simply click on the "Create New Memory" button on the homepage. Then, add a short text description, attach your media, and set the date, time, and location. Voilà! Your memory card is ready to bloom!

Can I choose who sees my memories?

Absolutely! Daily Diary allows you to customize the privacy settings for each memory card. You can choose to share your memories with specific groups of friends, family, or keep them entirely private. It's your garden, and you have full control!

Can I edit or delete a memory card after creating it?

Of course! We understand that memories may evolve over time. You can easily edit or delete a memory card whenever you like. Click on the memory card, select "Edit" or "Delete," and watch your garden transform!

How do I filter memories by date or location?

Exploring your memories is simple! Use our charming calendar view to filter memories by date. Click on any date, and memories recorded on that day will blossom before your eyes. To filter memories by location, just navigate to our delightful map view, and explore the memories pinned in different spots!

Can I share my memories on other social media platforms?

Absolutely! Daily Diary is all about spreading the joy. You can share your memory cards as captivating stories on popular social media platforms like Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, Snapchat, and more. Let your memories inspire others!

Is my data safe and secure on Daily Diary?

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use the latest encryption and security measures to protect your memories. Additionally, you have full control over who sees your memory cards. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.

How can I contact Daily Diary's support team?

We're always here to assist you! If you have any questions, feedback, or need support, you can reach out to us through the "Contact" section on our website. Our friendly team will be more than happy to help!

Is Daily Diary available on mobile devices?

Yes! Daily Diary is fully optimized for mobile devices. You can access and enjoy your memory garden on the go, capturing memories as they happen!

Can I invite friends to join Daily Diary?

Absolutely! We encourage you to invite your friends to join the Daily Diary community. Share your beautiful memories, inspire each other, and grow your memory gardens together!

If you have any more questions, feel free to explore our website or get in touch with our team. Happy doodling and sharing your memories with Daily Diary!
